Mainframe Computers

Mainframes are powerful machines designed to handle thousands of users at once. They are like a librarian helping hundreds of students simultaneously.

Characteristics:

  • Handles thousands of programs and users at the same time.
  • Large storage capacity and high reliability.
  • Used by banks, airlines, and government institutions.

Examples: IBM zSeries (keeps bank transactions secure)

Fun Fact: Mainframes can run continuously for years without stopping!
