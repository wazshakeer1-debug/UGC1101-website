Server Computers

Servers store, manage, and deliver data to other computers over a network. They work like a cafeteria that serves information to many people at once.

Characteristics:

  • Provide services such as websites and email 24/7.
  • Store and organize information for many users.
  • Support multiple devices at once.

Examples: Web servers, database servers.

Fun Fact: Servers can send website data to your device in milliseconds!
