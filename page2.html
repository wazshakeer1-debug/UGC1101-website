Supercomputers

Supercomputers are the most powerful computers in the world. They can perform billions of calculations per second and are used for massive tasks such as weather forecasting, climate modeling, and spacecraft simulation.

Characteristics:

  • Extremely fast using thousands of processors.
  • Very large and extremely expensive.
  • Used for scientific research and complex problem-solving.

Examples: Fugaku, Frontier.

Fun Fact: Frontier performs over 1 quintillion calculations per second!
